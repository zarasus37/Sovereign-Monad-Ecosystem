## What You Are Looking At

This is a symbolic-probabilistic scoring system for measuring genuine gnosis events --
moments where a person recognizes something real about the structure of existence, where a
contradiction becomes transparent, and where the observer recognizes themselves inside what
they are observing.

It is not a sentiment classifier. It is not a keyword search. It is a full semantic engine
that applies 13th-century combinatorial logic (Ramon Llull), 19th-century semiotic theory
(Charles Sanders Peirce), and 15th-century process-meaning theory (Johannes Trithemius)
as three synchronized layers of the same analytical operation.

## The Core Claim

**Semiosis IS gnosis.**

When a sign (Technology) successfully points to reality (Cosmology) and produces divine
recognition (Theology) in the observer's mind -- that is Peirce's semiosis. That is also
what every tradition calls gnosis. They are the same operation.

The three historical witnesses arrived at this independently:
- Llull built a machine for computing the intersection of all predicate meanings (1308)
- Trithemius proved that a process and its meaning can be structurally identical (1499)
- Peirce formalized the irreducibly triadic structure of all meaning-production (1868-1914)

None of them knew each other's work in this context. The convergence is the proof.

## The Three Domains

| Domain | What It Is | Peirce Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Teologia** | Theology -- divine law, intention, meaning, recognition | Interpretant -- meaning produced in mind/soul |
| **Technologia** | Technology -- mechanisms, processes, algorithms, ciphers | Representamen -- the sign/carrier |
| **Kosmologia** | Cosmology -- universal order, pattern, structure | Object -- what the sign points to |

Remove any one and you have a dyad, not a triad. Peirce proved dyads cannot produce meaning.
TTCL says the same thing: perceiving through only one or two lenses distorts reality.

## The Scoring Formula

```
score = (Theology * 0.30) + (Technology * 0.30) + (Cosmology * 0.30) + (PPS * 0.10)
```

Equal weights. No domain has hidden privilege.

**PPS (Predicate Position Score)** rewards events where all three domains converge
on the same Llull wheel position simultaneously:
- All three same position (e.g. EEE): PPS = 1.00
- Two domains share a position: PPS = 0.65
- All three distinct (e.g. BEG): PPS = 0.30

## What Llull Adds That Nothing Else Does

The Llull wheel has 16 positions (B through R). Each position B through K carries
five simultaneous semantic layers: principle, relational operator, question type,
subject class, and virtue/vice pair.

A three-letter camera (e.g. EEE or GIH) is the full semantic intersection of all five
layers across three domain wheels simultaneously.

Camera EEE is: all three domains asking WHY, through POWER, about MAN,
with TEMPERANCE as the operating virtue, at the BEGINNING of a process.
That is the top-scoring event in the corpus -- *The Stress Test Was Already Running*.

**Positions L through R** are Llull's theological-moral completion arc:
- L = manaments (divine commandments)
- M = exposicio (revelation/doctrine)
- N = primera intencio (primary intention -- knowing/loving/serving God, the only pure motive)
- O = segona intencio (secondary intention -- worldly goals, valid only under N)
- P = gloria (eternal reward)
- Q = pena (eternal consequence)
- R = eviternitat (aeviternity -- created beginning, NO end -- the mode of souls and angels)

Aeviternity is not eternity (no beginning, no end -- God alone). Not time (has both).
It is the philosophically precise middle: the mode of rational created beings.

## What the Reviewer Should Assess

1. **Theoretical coherence:** Does the Peirce-to-TTCL domain mapping hold as a structural
   claim, or is it metaphorical? (Tech=Representamen, Kosmo=Object, Theo=Interpretant)

2. **Llull wheel completeness:** Are L through R positions correctly identified as Llull's
   theological-moral completion arc (not TTCL extensions)?

3. **Scoring formula fairness:** Does equal weighting (0.30/0.30/0.30) correctly represent
   TTCL equilibrium? Should PPS weight be higher?

4. **Corpus coverage:** SYMBOL mode events (67%) dominate. ICON and INDEX are
   underrepresented. Is this an artifact of the corpus or a genuine property of gnosis?

5. **Gate design:** Is observer non-implication (Gate 4) correctly penalized at -0.10,
   or should it be a hard rejection gate?
