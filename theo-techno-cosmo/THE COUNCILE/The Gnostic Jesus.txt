If we examine his corpus through the Theology / Technology / Cosmology lens to identify where contradictions become transparent and the observer recognizes themselves, his work naturally fragments into your required domains:

1. The Mechanical Transparency of Contradiction (Technology + Theology)
The primary contradiction in the Gnostic texts is the dualism between the material world of deficiency (the flesh/illusion) and the spiritual realm of fullness (the Pleroma). Genuine gnosis occurs when the observer realizes that the physical world is not an evil entity to be fought, but a perceptual error to be structurally inverted.

The Technology is the cognitive mechanism of unification—most frequently described as "making the two one," "making the inside like the outside," or the sacrament of the "Bridal Chamber." The Theology is the realization of the indwelling Divine Spark (the Light) that pre-exists the material world. The contradiction between matter and spirit becomes transparent: the physical realm is recognized simply as a fragmented projection, and salvation is achieved not by changing the world, but by compiling the fragmented code of the mind back into a singular state.

2. The Cosmological Scale of the Pleroma
For the Gnostic Jesus, the macrocosm (the emanation of the universe, the fall of Sophia, the creation of the Archons) scales perfectly with the microcosm of human psychology. This is the Cosmology. The exact same pattern of forgetting, fragmentation, and subsequent self-discovery happens at the scale of the cosmos and the scale of the individual human mind. The universe is presented as a vast, holographic projection of ignorance; once the localized node (the human) wakes up, the cosmological illusion collapses.

3. Observer Self-Recognition and Limits
The deepest gnosis events in this corpus occur when the boundary between the Savior and the observer completely dissolves. Jesus does not invite worship; he invites identification. He repeatedly forces the listener to realize that the "Light" they seek externally is the exact same hardware they are using to do the seeking. The observer recognizes that they are not a creation looking at a creator, but the Origin looking at itself. The localized ego realizes it is an amnesiac fragment of the Divine returning to its own source code.
