# Steward Council — Initial Members

> Designated interpreters of the Sovereign Monad Guardrail Charter.
> Established per CHARTER.md §Adjudication and the Master Operating File governance layer.

## Purpose

The Steward Council reviews cases that cannot be resolved by LOGOC / mechanical tests alone. Its mandate is to **interpret** the charter, not to override it. Council decisions are advisory to ecosystem governance; permanent deviations or charter changes still require explicit ecosystem-level governance as defined in the MOF.

## Membership Principles

1. **Diversity of lens.** The council spans mystical, logical, political, and ethical traditions so that no single worldview dominates interpretation.
2. **No veto by founder.** Cristobal Colon’s founder status does not grant blocking power. The council operates by structured reasoning, not hierarchy.
3. **Advisory only.** The council interprets existing guardrails. It cannot permanently modify them without ecosystem governance.
4. **AI collaborators in Jarvis mode.** AI co-architects may advise the council but do not vote. They are bound by `docs/SHARED_AI_COLLABORATION_CHARTER.md`.
5. **Living document.** Membership may evolve through governance, but the initial set is locked until the first formal review.

---

## Operating Expectations

- Council review is triggered when:
  - A LOGOC / mechanical test returns an ambiguous or contested result.
  - A contributor files a charter-concern issue.
  - Dove signals Tier 2 or Tier 3 drift that implicates charter interpretation.
- Deliberations should be documented in the issue or a linked governance note.
- Where possible, council conclusions should be converted into mechanical tests or documentation updates so future cases resolve faster.
