simultaneously:

- **Theology** — meaning, will, origin, the divine attributes
- **Technology** — mechanism, structure, process, architecture

**The unifying principle:** Logic is not a fourth domain — it is the equilibrium state that
emerges when all three are held in simultaneous view. This framework is called
TheoTechnoCosmoLogic (TTCL) and its application to AI training is LOGOC.
