> Template only. Commercial rights are granted only through a signed written
> agreement, order form, statement of work, or other approved contract.

## 1. Definitions

- **Licensor:** The owner of The Sovereign Monad Ecosystem.
- **Licensee:** The individual or entity receiving a written commercial license.
- **Licensed Materials:** The modules, files, services, docs, deployment assets,
  or deliverables explicitly listed in the written agreement.
- **Excluded Materials:** Anything not expressly listed as Licensed Materials.
- **Production Use:** Any use outside internal evaluation, including live
  deployment, customer service, revenue generation, or operational reliance.

## 2. Evaluation Use

If granted in writing, Licensee may use the Licensed Materials for internal
evaluation only.

Evaluation use does not permit:

- production deployment
- redistribution
- sublicensing
- resale
- hosted-service use for third parties
- access to private alpha surfaces

## 3. Production Use

Production rights must be expressly granted in writing.

Unless the written agreement says otherwise, production rights are limited to:

- internal business use by Licensee
- deployment of only the listed Licensed Materials
- configuration and integration for Licensee's own environment
- no redistribution, resale, sublicensing, or third-party service use

## 4. Excluded Materials

The following remain excluded unless explicitly granted in writing:

- private alpha modules

- proprietary route ranking, timing, or fill-quality heuristics

- keys, secrets, customer records, and deployment credentials
- unpublished scoring, agent, model, or governance improvements

agreement explicitly says otherwise.
