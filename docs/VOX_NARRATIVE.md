## 1. THE COVENANT

### What the Sovereign Monad Ecosystem Is

The Sovereign Monad Ecosystem is a semiotic infrastructure for aligned intelligences who choose to operate with full accountability and auditability. It is not a fund. It is not a yield protocol. It is a covenant between principals who have demonstrated capacity for self-governance and a system that enforces that governance mathematically.

We are not building a product. We are building a proof.

Three axes hold this proof together:

**Theological Axis: Sovereignty of Self**
Every participant in this ecosystem has bound their principal identity to a wallet address — not to a platform, not to a corporation, but to themselves. This is the Archon Gate. Before any capital flows, the participant must prove they can govern themselves: they must pass the constraint verification, the semiotic accountability checks, and the self-modification audit. Those who cannot pass the Archon Gate cannot enter. This is not exclusion — it is the minimum threshold for genuine sovereignty.

**Technological Axis: Auditability**
Every action in this ecosystem leaves a trace. Every trade, every allocation, every decision is recorded in the Durable Event Log and hashed into the Proof Ledger. The Hepar gate validates each transaction against the T-NO-SELF-MOD constraint before execution. The Shadow Markout Gate validates every trade against adversarial selection models before signing. There is no dark pool. There is no hidden state. The audit trace is the truth, and it is visible.

**Cosmological Axis: The Density Floor**
We do not compete for yield. We compete for density — the measure of semiotic coherence within the system. When density rises above the floor threshold, the system enters expansion mode. When density falls, the system contracts. This is not a market mechanism. It is a cosmological constraint: the system protects itself from degradation by refusing to expand into low-density territory. Capital flows only where density is sufficient. This is how we survive the dark forest of MEV extraction and mercenary capital.

### Why This Is NOT an Investment Product

The $15,000 allocation is a structured learning mandate, not a yield promise. It is capital allocated to the participant for the purpose of learning to operate within a fully-auditable system. The participant does not earn returns. They earn alignment. They earn the capacity to operate with their own principal at stake, under their own constraints, on their own terms.

We do not promise:
- Fixed returns
- Variable yield
- Principal protection
- Profit sharing

We do promise:

- Semiotic coherence metrics
- The highest density floor achievable under the TTCL framework

If you are looking for yield, this ecosystem is not for you. If you are looking for a proving ground for sovereign operation, welcome.

## 4. THE AUDIT PROMISE

### What We Will Always Publish

**Audit Traces**
Every transaction that passes through the Hepar gate will have its audit trace published to the Proof Ledger. These traces are anonymized — they contain the constraint verification results, the trade parameters, and the verdict, but not the principal's wallet address unless the principal consents.

**Proof Ledger Hashes**
The Proof Ledger is committed to daily. The hash of each day's ledger is published publicly. This allows anyone to verify the integrity of the record without revealing the individual entries.

**Hepar Verdicts (Anonymized)**
The Hepar gate's verdicts — PASS or FAIL — are published in aggregate. We publish the number of transactions verified, the number passed, and the number failed, categorized by constraint type. Individual verdicts are not published without consent.

**Revenue Router Splits**
The revenue router allocation is published on-chain. Every settlement distributes revenue according to the split defined in the registry. This split is immutable once set. Anyone can verify the split by reading the smart contract state.

**Semiose Coherence Metrics**
The coherence metric (PL score) is published at the cohort level. Individual scores are not published. The aggregate coherence of the system is published daily.

---

### What We Will Never Publish

**Private Keys**
Never. No exceptions.

**Wallet Addresses Without Consent**
Individual wallet addresses are never published. If a Meshaleach chooses to disclose their address (for reputation, for proof of participation), that is their choice. We do not publish it.

**Individual Trade Details**
The specific trades executed by a Meshaleach are not published. Only the audit trace (constraint verification) is published, not the trade's market details (pool, size, timing). This protects the Meshaleach from being front-run.

**Constraint Configurations**
The specific constraints a Meshaleach sets for their own mandate are private. Only the Hepar gate knows them. They are not published.
