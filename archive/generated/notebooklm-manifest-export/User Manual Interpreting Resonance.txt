User Manual: Interpreting Resonance Scores
The Resonance Metric (R) determines the "3D Depth" of the result. Use this guide to adjudicate system outputs:
R > 0.90 (Focal Point):
Status: Durable Truth.
Action: High-confidence execution. Both lanes are perfectly aligned.
0.70 < R < 0.89 (Stable):
Status: High Integrity.
Action: Safe for analysis, though minor signal noise exists in the bitmask.
0.50 < R < 0.69 (Divergent):
Status: Blurred Vision.
Action: Proceed with caution. Indicates Lane A found an exploit that Lane B cannot fully verify.
R < 0.50 (Null Point):
Status: Blindness / Ambiguity Error.
Action: REJECT. The tracks are looking at different realities. Trigger "Blink" recovery.
